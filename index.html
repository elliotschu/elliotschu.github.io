Elliot Schumacher

I am a masters student (graduating in August 2017) at the Language Technologies Institute in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University. I work on the DSCoVAR project with Maxine Eskenazi, Kevyn Collins-Thompson and others. My most recent research focused on building a measure that ranks the difficulty of sentences for first-language readers using crowdsourced judgments (see publications below for more details).

Before attending CMU, I graduated from Ohio State University with a degree in both Computer Science and Linguistics. Additionally, I worked as a software developer for several years (as a student intern and later full-time).
